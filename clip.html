Core Concept

ClipVision is developing an affordable clip-on camera device that transforms regular glasses into smart vision assistance tools, controlled by a smartphone app.

An affordable solution that helps millions of people with visual impairments achieve independence.

Key Problems We Solve

  • High costs of current solutions (₹1,50,000 to ₹3,00,000).
  • Bulky and unattractive devices, discouraging usage.
  • Need for new hardware, increasing the cost of adoption.
  • Limited customization for different vision conditions.
  • Social isolation and safety concerns for visually impaired individuals.
  • Difficulty performing daily tasks like reading labels, shopping, etc.
  • Inadequate support for a wide range of visual impairments.

Our Solution

Hardware Features

  • Lightweight camera that clips onto existing glasses.
  • Wireless connection to a smartphone for easy control.
  • Long-lasting battery life (8+ hours).
  • Simple, user-friendly interface for effortless operation.
  • High-resolution camera with auto-focus and image stabilization.

Software Features

  • Smartphone app for real-time processing and enhancement.
  • Multiple vision modes tailored to different needs (e.g., reading, outdoor navigation).
  • Customizable settings based on doctor prescriptions.
  • Voice-activated commands for hands-free use.
  • Augmented reality (AR) capabilities for enhanced navigation.

Target Market

ClipVision addresses the needs of the 285 million visually impaired individuals worldwide, including:

  • Individuals with eye conditions like Albinism, AMD, Amblyopia, Astigmatism, Cataracts, and more.
  • Students and professionals who need vision support for tasks like reading and presentations.

Competitive Advantages

  • Affordable pricing (₹37,000-₹51,800 vs competitors' ₹1,50,000+).
  • Compatibility with any pair of glasses (no need for new eyewear).
  • Seamless smartphone integration for enhanced functionality.
  • Continuous software updates for feature improvement.
  • Doctor-connected platform for easy sharing of data and management of vision conditions.
  • User-centric design based on feedback from individuals with vision impairments.

Revenue Model

Our revenue will primarily come from:

  • Direct sales of ClipVision units through online and retail channels.
  • Subscription-based services for app features (e.g., advanced vision modes, cloud storage, and updates).
  • Partnerships with healthcare providers for patient-specific packages.
  • Advertising revenue from in-app notifications for vision-related products and services.

Market Size

The global market for vision assistance technology is rapidly growing, with an estimated:

  • 285 million visually impaired individuals worldwide.
  • Potential reach in the assistive tech market of over $15 billion by 2025.
  • India’s assistive tech market alone expected to grow by 20% annually.

Our initial focus will be on India, where the need for affordable assistive devices is significant.

Executive Summary

ClipVision is revolutionizing the vision assistance market by providing an affordable, customizable, and user-friendly solution for visually impaired individuals. Our clip-on camera technology, paired with a powerful mobile app, makes everyday tasks like reading, navigation, and social interactions easier for those with visual impairments.

Value Proposition

This drive fuels my passion for making ClipVision a reality.
